Sense Cables

Digital Water and Bases Sense Cables

FG-EC

FG-EC digital sense cable detects with localization the presence of water and bases in metric mode. It is connected to the FG-SYS digital unit. It exists in three lengths of 3, 7 and 15 metres.

Digital Acids Sense Cables

FG-AC

FFG-AC digital sense cable detects with localisation the presence of acid in metric mode. It is connected to the FG-SYS digital unit. It exists in three lengths of 3, 7 and 15 metres.

Water and Bases Sector Sense Cables

FG-ECS

FG-ECS digital sense cable detects with localisation the presence of water and bases in sector mode. It is connected to the FG-SYS digital unit via a FG-DTCS box; or connected to the FG-A alarm directly. It exists in three lengths of 3, 7 and 15 metres.

Water and Bases Sector Sense Cables

FG-ECX

FG-ECX sense cable detects without localisation the presence of water and bases in metric mode. It is connected to the FG-A alarm directly. It exists in three lengths of 3, 7 and 15 metres.

Digital Hydrocarbons and Solvents Sense Cables

FG-HC2

FG-HC2 digital sensing cable detects and localizes the presence of hydrocarbons and solvents. Connected to one of the modular central FG-A alarm or FG-SYS digital units. It exists in a standard length of 2 m.
